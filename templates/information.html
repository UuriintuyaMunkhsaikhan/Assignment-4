Welcome to GIC Student Progress Assessment

Information

The GIC Student Progress Assessment serves as a valuable tool for evaluating various aspects of students' academic performance and overall experience at Glasgow International College. By collecting comprehensive data through this assessment, several benefits can be observed:

Benefits

  • Improves academic programs based on student feedback.
  • Enhances overall student experience and satisfaction.
  • Helps in identifying areas for improvement.

FAQs

What is the purpose of this assessment?
The primary purpose is to track the academic journey of students and improve the overall academic experience.
How often is the data collected?
The data is collected periodically to ensure accurate and up-to-date insights.
Is the data confidential?
Yes, all data collected is kept confidential and is used solely for academic improvement purposes.
